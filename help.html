Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I edit my profile?

    Go to Your Profile → click "Edit Profile", then save your changes.

  • I forgot my password. What can I do?

    Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.

  • How can I delete my account?

    Send a request to support@example.com with your username.

  • Is my personal data secure?

    Yes — your password is encrypted and never displayed or shared. Only essential profile data is visible.
